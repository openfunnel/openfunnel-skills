# Competitor Sales Rep Spy Agent

**Agent name:** Spy on your Competitor's Sales Reps in Real-time

**What it does:** Scans a competitor sales rep's LinkedIn activity to surface which accounts they are actively engaging with. Alerts you when competitors enter your territory.

---

## Input Format

**Input:** Individual competitor LinkedIn profile URL only. Must be a person, not a company page.

**Max Pages:** Controls scan depth — increase for more thorough monitoring.

---

## Output

Accounts being engaged by the competitor rep, auto-qualified against your ICP.

---

## How to Use

1. Identify your top 3-5 competitors' outbound sales reps on LinkedIn.
2. Set up a spy agent for each rep.
3. Cross-reference surfaced accounts with your pipeline:
   - **Account in your pipeline** = competitive deal, accelerate.
   - **Account not in your pipeline** = net-new expansion opportunity.
4. Accounts appearing across multiple competitor spy agents = hot market segment, high urgency.

---

## Signal Interpretation

| Competitor Behavior | Interpretation |
|--------------------|---------------|
| Rep actively engaging with an account | Account is in active evaluation/buying cycle |
| Rep engagement drops off on an account | Deal may have fallen through — warm takeout window |
| Multiple competitor reps engaging same account | Competitive bake-off in progress |
| Rep engaging with accounts in a new vertical | Competitor expanding into new market — get there first |
