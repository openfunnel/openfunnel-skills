# Job Change Monitoring Agent

**Agent name:** Monitor all ICP people at qualified companies changing jobs

**What it does:** Tracks LinkedIn for ICP professionals who recently joined new companies. Filters out internal promotions — only surfaces genuine new hires.

---

## Input

Configured at the campaign level — monitors ICP-matching people across qualified accounts for job changes.

## Output

People who recently started at a new company, with role, company, and timing data.

---

## Why New Hires Are a Top Signal

A person hired to solve a problem has a concentrated buying window:

| Timeframe | Behavior | Outreach Readiness |
|-----------|----------|-------------------|
| Week 1-2 | Onboarding, learning landscape | Not ready |
| Week 3-6 | Evaluating what exists, identifying gaps | Prime window |
| Month 2-3 | Making vendor decisions, getting budget approval | Closing window |
| Month 4+ | Decisions made, stack chosen | Window closed |

**Rule:** The 2-month window from job posted → person hired is the buying window. A new hire has 90-day pressure to pick vendors and show early wins.

---

## Hiring Pattern Interpretation

| Pattern | What It Means |
|---------|--------------|
| New VP/Director hire | Strategic mandate from above, budget pre-approved |
| New Head of [function] at company with no existing team | Blank slate buyer, picking every tool from scratch |
| New CRO/VP Sales + SDR hiring surge | Revenue scale-up, needs entire sales tooling stack |
| New CISO/Head of Security | Compliance/security tooling evaluation, often board-mandated timeline |

---

## Key Distinction

This agent filters internal moves. A promotion from Senior Engineer → Staff Engineer at the same company is not a signal. A Staff Engineer joining a new company IS a signal — they bring budget authority and mandate to build.
