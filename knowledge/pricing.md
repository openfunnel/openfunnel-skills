# Credit Economics & Value Pricing


## Credit Economics & Value Pricing

### The Pricing Philosophy

"OpenFunnel charges you only for qualified, high-fit, signal-rich accounts and prospects that you can reach out to with context, today."

### Credit Costs
- **Company import:** 3 credits (full enrichment included)
- **Person import:** 1 credit per LinkedIn profile
- **Account signal:** 10 credits (net-new ICP-qualified account with actionable trigger)
- **People signal:** 20 credits (people-level activity discovery)
- **ICP People Finder:** 6 credits per person returned
- **Micro-audience creation:** 0.5 credits per row
- **Daily agent run:** 20 credits fixed daily cost
- **Email verification:** 1 credit (includes deliverability status)
- **Phone numbers:** 10 credits

### Pricing Tiers
- **Free:** Basic features, limited support, no credits
- **Blaze ($700/mo):** 20,000 credits, all Pro Agents, Apollo & Clay integration, LinkedIn enrichment, Agentic Message Builder, Slack support
- **Advanced ($1,000/mo):** 50,000 credits, in-account monitoring, 2-way CRM sync, white-glove onboarding, founder Slack support
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing

### The Value Equation

Signals must contain at least one actionable signal (hiring, expansion, stack change, compliance push) to qualify for credit charge. No charge for noise.
